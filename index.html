Z+ Security collects only the minimum data required to operate moderation and security features. This may include:

The bot may temporarily process message content to detect spam, phishing links, abusive language, or rule violations. Messages are analyzed in real time for moderation purposes and are not permanently stored unless required for moderation logs.
